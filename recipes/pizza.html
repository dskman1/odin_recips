Margherita Pizza Recipe

Margherita Pizza Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Margherita Pizza Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a flat circle.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese.
  5. Place fresh basil leaves on top.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza.
  7. Bake for about 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden.
  8. Remove from the oven, slice, and serve.
