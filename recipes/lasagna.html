Lasagna

Lasagna Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Lasagna Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté chopped onion and garlic.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Spread a layer of sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  9. Top with parmesan cheese.
  10. Bake for about 30–40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
