Chicken Shawarma

Chicken Shawarma Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thighs
  • Garlic
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pita bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic sauce (or tahini)

Chicken Shawarma Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into thin slices.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and spices.
  3. Add the chicken to the marinade and mix well.
  4. Let the chicken marinate for at least 1 hour.
  5. Cook the chicken in a pan over medium heat until fully cooked.
  6. Warm the pita bread.
  7. Add the cooked chicken to the bread.
  8. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, and garlic sauce.
  9. Wrap the bread and serve.
