# Odin Recipes

Simple recipes for three dishes: Margherita Pizza, Lasagna, and Chicken Shawarma.

---

## 🍕 Margherita Pizza

**Ingredients**

* Pizza dough
* Tomato sauce
* Mozzarella
* Basil
* Olive oil
* Salt

**Instructions**

1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
2. Roll out dough and spread tomato sauce.
3. Add mozzarella and basil.
4. Drizzle olive oil.
5. Bake 10–15 minutes.

---

## 🍝 Lasagna

**Ingredients**

* Lasagna noodles
* Ground beef
* Tomato sauce
* Ricotta
* Mozzarella
* Parmesan

**Instructions**

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Cook noodles.
3. Brown beef and add sauce.
4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese.
5. Bake 30–40 minutes.

---

## 🌯 Chicken Shawarma

**Ingredients**

* Chicken
* Yogurt
* Garlic
* Lemon juice
* Olive oil
* Spices
* Pita bread
* Lettuce
* Tomato

**Instructions**

1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, garlic, lemon, oil, and spices.
2. Cook chicken in a pan.
3. Place in pita with lettuce and tomato.
4. Wrap and serve.
